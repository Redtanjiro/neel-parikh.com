A little
about me.

I'm Neel — an interaction & immersive designer based in Sydney, finishing my Master of Design at UNSW.

I like building things that feel made by a person: hand-painted interfaces, small warm interactions, brand worlds you can walk around in.

— Neel

journey.

Now in Sydney for my Master's, working across product, brand, and immersive design.

And AI tools woven through it all — as a collaborator, not a crutch.
