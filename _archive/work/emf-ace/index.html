01 Overview

One brand world, from screen to sand.

EMF ACE was EMF Global's biggest moment yet — a three-day flagship in Abu Dhabi marking eleven years, hosting industry leaders, celebrity speakers, and luxury partners at venues like Emirates Palace and the Ritz-Carlton.

As design lead, I built the identity and ran the campaign end to end: a cohesive, high-glam brand system that had to hold together across dozens of formats and land as effortlessly on a phone screen as on a gala stage.

The brief

Give a milestone event a world-class, luxurious identity — one that fused Formula 1 energy, Arabian grandeur, and EMF's legacy, and stayed coherent across social, print, environment, and stage.

The approach

A single art direction — deep purple and gold, editorial type, cinematic desert imagery — applied as a flexible system, so every touchpoint felt unmistakably ACE while flexing to its format.

02 The Brand System

A luxury language: purple, gold, and grandeur.

At the centre sat the ACE mark and a commemorative "11 Glorious Years" lockup. Around them, a disciplined system — a royal purple and gold palette for opulence, an editorial serif-and-sans type pairing for authority, and cinematic desert-meets-city imagery as the visual signature.

03 How We Worked

A simple, creativity-driven process — repeated across every asset.

Every post and piece moved through the same rhythm — understand the message, explore ways to express it visually, then execute with clarity and polish.

01

Ideation

Understand what each piece needs to say — a brand message, a feature, a call to action — then explore creative ways to express it.

02

Execution

Craft each design to be both captivating and clear, delivering impact and beauty that resonates with the audience.

03

Integration

Fold sponsor brands into the content so they align with ACE's identity, colours, and style — cohesive, never bolted on.

04

Consistency

Hold one visual voice across social, print, environment, and stage — so everything reads as a single world.

04 The Campaign

A rollout that built anticipation, post by post.

The campaign moved in acts. Teasers set the mood — a magic lamp, a flying carpet, opportunities waiting beyond the dunes — each hinting at the event without naming it. Then the tempo picked up: a one-week countdown, an early-bird deadline, a "500+ registered" milestone, and the call for award nominations. Every post spoke the same luxury language while carrying its own moment in the story.

05 Speakers & Partners

Star power, framed in gold.

The line-up demanded designs worthy of its names — from method-actor Nawazuddin Siddiqui to the Global Design Fusion panel, each speaker got a bespoke card in the ACE language. Luxury partners — the Ritz-Carlton, Emirates Palace — were integrated with the same care, their brands sitting inside ACE's world rather than beside it.

06 The Event, Live

Where the brand left the screen.

Across three days at Abu Dhabi's most extraordinary venues, the identity came to life — chandelier installations spelling EMF, a gala dinner under a fireworks-lit dome, the ACE stage, and branded touchpoints down to the menu on every plate at Emirates Palace.
